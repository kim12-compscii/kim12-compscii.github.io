About This Little Cafe

At This Little Cafe, we believe everyone deserves a delicious cup of coffee made from top-quality beans. We proudly serve fresh coffee from Velvet Sunrise Coffee Roasters and pair it with locally baked goods. Supporting our community has been our passion since 2010.
